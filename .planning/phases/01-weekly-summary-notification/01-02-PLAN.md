<objective>
Implement `WeeklySummaryService` — the `Services/` layer notification scheduler
for the weekly summary — mirroring `ReminderService`'s exact shape (stable
identifier, pure trigger factory, idempotent schedule/cancel,
`@AppStorage`-gated `scheduleIfEnabled`), but sourcing its dynamic notification
body from `WeeklySummaryCalculator` (plan 01-01) and fetching current/prior
week `ConsumptionEvent`s directly via SwiftData `FetchDescriptor`.

Purpose: this is the single place ENGG-03 (weekly-repeating, locale-aware,
9am-local trigger) and the correctness-critical "always use physical
`pureAlcoholGrams`, never mode-density" domain rule get implemented and tested
before any UI wires into it.
Output: `Services/WeeklySummaryService.swift` with full unit coverage.
</objective>
