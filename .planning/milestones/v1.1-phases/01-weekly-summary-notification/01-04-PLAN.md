Settings card (`WeeklySummarySection`, mirroring `ReminderSection`) and a second, independent

added in plan 01-01, and both stay fully independent of the Health/Reminder toggles they sit
beside per D-06.

Purpose: this is the only user-visible way to turn the feature on; without it, ENGG-01/02 (and
therefore the whole phase goal) cannot be exercised by a real user.
Output: one new SwiftUI component, two edited files.
</objective>
