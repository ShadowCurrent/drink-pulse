## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-01-06 | Tampering | `NotificationActionHandler.didReceive`'s identifier match | low | accept | All identifiers are locally generated (by `WeeklySummaryService`/`ReminderService`) and locally consumed — no server-issued payload exists to spoof; the existing if/else-if structure already silently ignores any unrecognized identifier, which is the correct, unchanged mitigation |
| T-01-07 | Elevation of Privilege | Tap-triggered tab switch | low | accept | The only possible side effect of any notification identifier match is selecting an existing, already-visible tab (`.insights`) — no privileged action, no data mutation, no navigation outside the app's own normal tab set |
